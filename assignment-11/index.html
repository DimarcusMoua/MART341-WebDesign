Playstation controllers vs Xbox controllers

Within the last few decades we have seen some awesome developments in gaming. For example we've seen the Playstation one evolve through the Playstation five along with Xbox evolving from the Xbox to the Xbox Series S. One could argue that these two amazing series of consoles have rivaled each other for years and still continue to. A key difference to me about the current consoles are the controllers. Over the years we have seen console after console come out but it seemed as if the controllers only changed a little bit. This can be seen less in Xbox nowadays as they have different controllers that have different buttons and analog sticks that can be attactched to one. Playstation, on the other hand, seemed to have similar controllers for years, at least to me.
