The Game

The sport itself is relatively easy to understand. The field itself is about 110 to 120 yards in length and 75 yards wide. Here is a diagram of the field demensions. 22 players then fill the field with 11 for each team.

Gameplay

Teams can organize in various formations. Some formations that are popular are: 4-4-2, 4-3-2-1 and 4-5-1. These formations can also be played with. For example the formation can be wider, longer in depth. This can change the attacking and defending strategy for each team. Positions for teams can be:

  • Goalkeeper
  • Left/Right outside defensive back
  • Left/Right center denfensive back
  • Left/Right outside attacking wingers
  • Center attacking/defending midfielders
  • Left/Right attacking forwards or strikers
