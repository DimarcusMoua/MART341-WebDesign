What we do for Fun

When the guys all get together, we often go bowling. We go so often that we know some of the workers at the bowling alley. If bowling sounds too exhausting, we end up at Canyon's house where we watch movies, watch our own video game clips, or cook. In the summer, we tend to go to the Big Dipper for ice cream. Lastly, just about every night we squeeze in a few hours of gaming. On our Playstations we mainly play Rocket League, Rust Console Edition, Grand Theft Auto IV, Need for Speed Heat, and Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege.
