Hmong Missoula

Missoula has a special place in many Hmong hearts as this valley was called home for many. From the mid 70's through the 80's, many Hmong people were desperate to leave SouthEast Asia after the vietnam war. In partnership of the CIA, many Hmong refugees were relocated within the United States, Missoula being one of the first spots.
